Who We Are

ChatHealthy.ai is an AI-native application that helps consumers find appropriate healthcare services. It is operated by Skip Snow, an individual US citizen.

ChatHealthy.ai is capable of HIPAA compliance and can be deployed within a HIPAA context without modification, but doing so would require a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with any covered entity. See Legal Status & HIPAA below.

What We Collect

Contact information — When you choose to share your name and email, we retain it solely to follow up on your inquiry.

Conversation data — When you provide contact information, we ask for consent to store your conversation:

  • Verbatim consent: your complete conversation is stored with your contact record.
  • Summary consent only (if verbatim is declined): an AI-generated, de-identified summary is stored instead. No identifying information is retained.
  • No consent: your contact information only. No conversation stored.

Unanswered questions — Regardless of consent, when ChatHealthy.ai cannot answer a question, it records the question and a de-identified version of the conversation leading to it. These records contain no personally identifying information and are used solely to improve ChatHealthy.ai's capabilities.

ChatHealthy.ai does not attempt to infer or reverse-engineer the identity of any user — registered or anonymous — from any data it holds.

How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your inquiries.
  • To improve ChatHealthy.ai's capabilities (de-identified question data only).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party beyond the vendors listed below, and only for the purpose of delivering this service.

Legal Status & HIPAA

ChatHealthy.ai is operated by Skip Snow as an individual US citizen and is currently structured as a website and technology solution — not as a HIPAA Covered Entity or Business Associate.

ChatHealthy.ai does not hold a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) — a contract governing how protected health information may be handled — with any of its vendors, and is therefore subject only to each vendor's general terms of service.

ChatHealthy.ai is committed to handling all health-related information with care, discretion, and ethical responsibility that goes beyond what any legal obligation alone would require.
